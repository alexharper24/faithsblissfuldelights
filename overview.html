Meet the Baker
"The best treats are made by hand, from scratch, and shared with the people you love."

Faith's Blissful Delights began in a home kitchen with a love of baking and a wish to make people's celebrations a little sweeter. What started as treats for family and friends grew into custom orders for birthdays, weddings, holidays, and every reason to celebrate in between. Each one is still made by hand, to order, with the same care that started it all.
